Blueberry oat pancakes
======================

Via Alex's mum.  Serves 4.

Ingredients
-----------

-   2 bananas
-   3 medium eggs
-   70g rolled oats
-   50–100g blueberries
-   ½tsp salt
-   Butter

Process
-------

1.  Blend bananas, eggs, oats and salt until smooth.
2.  Melt butter in a pan, pour on pancake batter, scatter on blueberries.
